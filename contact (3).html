Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

A few answers to questions we hear most often. If yours isn't here, just ask.

How much does a typical project cost?
Every project is different — cost depends on scope, materials, location, and timeline. We always provide a detailed, itemised quote before any work begins, so you'll know exactly where every kwacha goes. Our first consultation is completely free.
How long does a typical build take?
A single-room renovation can take 2–4 weeks. A full residential build typically runs 3–8 months depending on scale and complexity. We provide a project timeline at the proposal stage and keep to it — our on-time delivery record is 100%.
Do you work outside of Lusaka?
Yes. While we are headquartered in Lusaka, we take on projects across Zambia. Travel and accommodation costs for our team would be factored into the project quote for locations outside the city.
Do I need to have a design before approaching you?
Not at all. Many of our clients come to us with just a rough idea or inspiration images. Our design team will work with you to develop a full brief and concept before any construction begins.
What is your payment structure?
We work on a staged payment model tied to project milestones — typically a deposit to commence, then payments linked to key stages of completion. Full details are always outlined in the project agreement.
Will I have a single point of contact throughout?
Yes. Every project is assigned a dedicated project manager who will be your primary contact from kickoff to handover. You won't be passed around — we know how frustrating that is.
