Common Questions

Answers You Need Before Starting

Do you work on projects outside Lusaka?
Yes. While most of our work is concentrated in Lusaka, we regularly take on projects in Copperbelt, Livingstone, and other major Zambian cities. Travel and accommodation for our team is factored into the project proposal.
How do you price your services?
Every project is unique, so we provide detailed, itemised quotations rather than fixed rates. After an initial consultation, we prepare a full scope of works with transparent cost breakdowns. There are no hidden fees.
How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by service and scale. A single-room remodel typically takes 2–4 weeks. A full house build can range from 4–12 months. We provide a detailed programme at proposal stage and stick to it.
Can I see my project design before work begins?
Absolutely. We don't begin any physical work until you've reviewed and approved the design concept, material selections, and cost plan. You control the pace — we guide the process.
What makes Bespoke Designs different?
We're not a large contractor that assigns you to a junior project officer. You work directly with Samuel and our senior team throughout. That means faster decisions, consistent quality, and a relationship that lasts beyond the project.
